DAY-National Rural Livelihood Mission

  1. A flagship poverty alleviation program implemented by the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India.

  2. Social mobilization, strengthening of self-managed and financially sustainable community institutions of the rural poor women

  3. Financial inclusion and sustainable livelihoods

  4. Social inclusion, social development and access to entitlements through convergence.

  5. The program seeks to reach out to around 10 Crore rural poor households in a phased manner by 2022-23.

National Food Security Mission (NFSM)

  1. In view of the stagnating food grain production and an increasing consumption need of the growing population, Government of India has launched its Centrally Sponsored Scheme, ‘National Food Security Mission’ in October 2007.

  2. The Mission continued during 12th Five Year Plan with new targets of additional production of food grains of 25 million tonnes of food grains comprising of 10 million tonnes rice, 8 million tonnes of wheat, 4 million tonnes of pulses and 3 million tonnes of coarse cereals by the end of 12th Five Year Plan.
